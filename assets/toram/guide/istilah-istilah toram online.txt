📊 STATISTIK KARAKTER (STATS)

- *ATK* = Attack (Serangan Fisik)
- *MATK* = Magic Attack (Serangan Sihir)
- *DEF* = Defense (Pertahanan Fisik)
- *MDEF* = Magic Defense (Pertahanan Sihir)
- *HIT* = Hit Rate (Akurasi)
- *FLEE* = Flee Rate (Menghindar)
- *ASPD* = Attack Speed (Kecepatan Serang)
- *CSPD* = Cast Speed (Kecepatan Merapal)
- *AMPR* = Attack MP Recovery (Pemulihan MP per Basic Attack)
- *LV* = Level
- *EXP* = Experience Points (Pengalaman)
- *HP* = Health Points (Darah)
- *MP* = Magic Points (Mana)
- *STR* = Strength (Kekuatan)
- *INT* = Intelligence (Kecerdasan)
- *VIT* = Vitality (Daya Hidup)
- *AGI* = Agility (Kelincahan)
- *DEX* = Dexterity (Ketangkasan)
- *CD* = Critical Damage
- *CR* = Critical Rate
- *PP* = Physical Pierce (Penetrasi Fisik)
- *SRD* = Short Range Damage (Damage Jarak Dekat)
- *LRD* = Long Range Damage (Damage Jarak Jauh)
- *DTE* = Damage To Element (Damage berdasarkan elemen)
- *RTE* = Resist To Element (Tahanan elemen)
- *Crit Res* = Critical Resistance (Tahanan Kritis)
- *Aggro / AGR* = Agravation / Threat (Tingkat kebencian musuh)
- *Prorate* = Penurunan/kenaikan damage serangan berdasarkan jenis serangan

⚔️ PERTARUNGAN (BATTLE)

- *Buff* = Efek positif
- *Debuff* = Efek negatif
- *Interrupt* = Skill dengan efek Flinch, Tumble, Stun, Knock Back (mengganggu boss)
- *FTS* = Flinch, Tumble & Stun
- *AOE* = Area of Effect (serangan area)
- *DOT* = Damage Over Time (serangan berkelanjutan)

- *MK* = Mass Kill (kumpulkan & bunuh banyak mini boss)

- *Leech* = Pemain numpang tanpa bantu apa-apa
- *Ally* = Sekutu (gabungan 1+ party)
- *Raid Boss* = Boss yang bisa dihadapi >1 party
- *Diff* = Difficulty (Normal, Hard, Nightmare, Ultimate)
- *Gem Run* = Pakai permata khusus untuk naikkan drop rate boss
- *STD* = Stoodie
- *NG* = No Gem
- *FG* = Full Gem
- *Cap* = Level maksimal
- *Drop* = Barang yang jatuh dari musuh
- *Merc* = Mercenary (Bodyguard yang disewa)
- *OOF* = Mati (dari suara Roblox)
- *R/RD* = Ready (siap)
- *FC* = Full Critical / Force Close (tertutup paksa) – tergantung konteks
